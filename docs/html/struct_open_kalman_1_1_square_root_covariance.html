template<typename Coeffs, typename ArgType>
struct OpenKalman::SquareRootCovariance< Coeffs, ArgType >

The upper or lower triangle Cholesky factor (square root) of a covariance matrix. If S is a SquareRootCovariance, S*S.transpose() is a Covariance.
