template<typename C1, typename C2, typename C3>
struct OpenKalman::Spherical< C1, C2, C3 >

A group reflecting spherical coordinates.

Coefficient1, Coefficient2, and Coefficient3 must be some combination of Distance, Inclination, and Angle/angle::Radians, reflecting the distance, inclination, and azimuth, respectively. Examples: Spherical<Distance, Inclination, angle::Radians>, Spherical<angle::Radians, Distance, Inclination>.

Template Parameters

Coefficient2Distance, inclination, or Angle (e.g., angle::Radians).
Coefficient3Distance, inclination, or Angle (e.g., angle::Radians).
